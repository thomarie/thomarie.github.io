Gaming & Coding Class at JHS

Some Things You'll Learn About

  • Psychology of Gaming
  • Programming Languages
  • Creating Reviews
  • Building your own Games
  • ...And so much more!

Standards and Skills Focus

The principals of the class reflect the concept of learning goals that allow students to develop their ability with new skills.

Skills and learning goals are tackled on multiple occassions throughout the semester in order for students to practice the skills and achieve mastery.

Learning Outcomes

Students will develop a greater understanding of the creation of video games; develop a greater understanding of the video game industry (from the business angle); db competent creators in multiple coding languages; be able to transfer skills from one platform to another; be able to think critically about what and why they, and other people, play video games; develop a greater understanding of the impacts of video games on society; become more knowledgeable in the video game, coding, and computer science fields of study

How to Enroll in the Class

  1. Meet with your guidance counselor
  2. Tell them, "I have just got to take that cool sounding Gaming & Coding class."
  3. Get psyched up by the fact that you are now enrolled in the coolest class on campus!
